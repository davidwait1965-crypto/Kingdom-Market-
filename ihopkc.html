IHOPKC – A Culture of Night and Day Prayer

A simple look at the heart behind the International House of Prayer in Kansas City and why 24/7 worship still matters today.

Prayer · Worship · Presence of God

IHOPKC (International House of Prayer Kansas City) is known around the world as a community built around one main thing: continual worship and prayer. Since 1999, teams of worshippers and intercessors have been lifting up the name of Jesus in prayer rooms filled with music, Scripture, and simple hunger for God.

What Is IHOPKC All About?

At its core, IHOPKC is a missions base centered on night-and-day prayer. Worship teams and prayer leaders rotate through the prayer room in live two‑hour sets, creating a rhythm of songs, Scripture, and intercession that continues around the clock.

The heartbeat of IHOPKC:
  • Hosting God's presence through worship and prayer
  • Standing in the gap for cities, nations, and the Church
  • Equipping believers to love Jesus with their whole lives
  • Sending missionaries and prayer carriers into the world

More Than a Worship Set

While the live worship sets are what many people first see, life at IHOPKC includes teaching, discipleship programs, internships, Bible courses, and community life. Young and old gather to grow in the Word, hear God's heart, and live lifestyles of prayer and mission.

Why 24/7 Prayer?

IHOPKC takes its model from biblical pictures of continual worship— from King David's tabernacle to the heavenly worship scenes in Revelation. The belief is simple: Jesus is worthy of unceasing adoration, and continual prayer softens hearts and shifts atmospheres in ways we may never fully see.

How It Connects with Kingdom Market

Kingdom Market loves highlighting ministries like IHOPKC because they remind us of what truly matters: God's presence, His Word, and a lifestyle of worship. As we champion faith-filled creativity, resources, and community, places of prayer like IHOPKC serve as spiritual “wells” that refresh and fuel the wider body of Christ.
