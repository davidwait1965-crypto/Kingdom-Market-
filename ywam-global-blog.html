YWAM – A Global Family on Mission

A snapshot of Youth With A Mission around the world: ordinary people saying “yes” to an extraordinary God.

Global · Missions · Worship

Youth With A Mission (YWAM) is a global movement of believers who want to know God and make Him known. From major cities to remote villages, YWAMers are serving, preaching the gospel, and demonstrating the love of Jesus.

A Family from Many Nations

One of the most beautiful parts of YWAM is its diversity—people of different ages, cultures, and backgrounds coming together around the Great Commission. It’s less about an organization and more about a family on mission.

Training, Outreach, and Mercy

Across the world, YWAM focuses on three main areas: training, evangelism, and mercy ministries. Discipleship Training Schools (DTS) are one of the primary on-ramps—intensive seasons of learning and then going.

Why Kingdom Market Highlights YWAM

At Kingdom Market, we love YWAM because it reflects so much of our own heart: faith in action, creativity, and a desire to see every sphere of society touched by the gospel.
