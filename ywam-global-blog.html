A Global Family on Mission for Jesus

Youth With A Mission (YWAM) isn’t just an organization—it’s a movement of believers from every corner of the earth who share one passion: knowing God and making Him known.

For over 60 years, YWAM has been equipping and sending out thousands of Christians to:

  • Preach the Gospel in unreached nations
  • Serve the poor through mercy ministries
  • Train disciples who will transform their communities
  • Impact every sphere of society with Kingdom values

From crowded urban streets to isolated villages, YWAMers are living out the Great Commission—and you can too.

Why Thousands Are Saying “Yes” to YWAM

Three Ways YWAM is Transforming Nations

Training & Discipleship

Equipping believers to live out their calling

YWAM’s Discipleship Training Schools (DTS) are life-changing experiences where you will:

  • Study God’s Word intensively
  • Discover your identity and calling
  • Learn to hear God’s voice
  • Prepare for cross-cultural ministry

Duration: Typically 5–6 months (lecture phase + outreach phase)

Evangelism & Church Planting

Taking the Gospel to the ends of the earth

YWAM teams are actively:

  • Sharing Jesus among unreached people groups
  • Planting churches in pioneer areas
  • Leading worship and revival gatherings
  • Discipling new believers

Impact: Millions have encountered Christ through YWAM outreach.

Mercy Ministries

Demonstrating God’s love through action

YWAM serves the vulnerable through:

  • Medical missions and healthcare
  • Orphan care and family support
  • Disaster relief and humanitarian aid
  • Education and community development

Mission: Show the world that Jesus cares for the whole person—body, soul, and spirit.

Ready to Step Into Your Calling?

God is calling you to be part of this global movement. Whether you’re 18 or 80, whether you go for 3 months or 3 years, YWAM has a place for you.

Here’s how to get started:

  • Step 1: Pray & Explore – Ask God if He’s calling you to missions. Visit YWAM.org to learn about programs, locations, and opportunities worldwide.
  • Step 2: Choose Your Program – DTS (Discipleship Training School) for first-timers, short-term outreach (2 weeks to 3 months), long-term missions (1+ years of service), or specialized schools such as worship, media, sports, business, and more.
  • Step 3: Apply & Prepare – Connect with a YWAM base, complete your application, and begin fundraising. YWAM staff will walk with you through every step.
  • Step 4: Go & Transform – Step into the adventure God has for you and watch Him work through you in ways you never imagined.
