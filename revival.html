University of Tennessee Baptism Revival

Hearts Turning Back to the Father

The University of Tennessee recently witnessed a remarkable spiritual breakthrough, resulting in the baptism of more than 300 young people. In a world increasingly consumed by constant clicking and scrolling, this moment stands as a powerful reminder that the love and grace of the Father still break through and transform lives.

Our mission at Kingdom Market is to continue sharing God’s message, spreading the gospel, and creating opportunities for His truth to reach hearts everywhere — through story, testimony, and faith-inspired creativity.
