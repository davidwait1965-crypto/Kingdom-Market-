Discovering YWAM Salem — Where Adventure Meets Faith

A fun, down-to-earth look at life, calling, and missions at YWAM Salem in Oregon.

Faith · Missions · Community
By Kingdom Market · Sharing stories of what God is doing around the world.

If you’ve ever wondered what it looks like when faith, fun, and purpose collide... YWAM Salem is the place.

It feels like a mix of summer camp, Bible school, and adventure with God — all rolled into one.

So… What Is YWAM?

YWAM stands for Youth With A Mission — a global Christian movement with one heartbeat: to know God and make Him known.

What Happens at YWAM Salem?

1. Training That Changes You

Students step into a focused season of learning, worship, discipleship, and hearing God’s voice.

2. Outreach That Impacts Others

After training, students travel to nations to serve, love, and bring the gospel in practical ways.

3. Everyday Community Life

Prayer, meals, learning, serving — real life together that transforms hearts.

Loved about YWAM Salem:
  • Authentic community
  • Growing deeper with God
  • Discovering purpose and calling
  • Experiencing cultural missions

Common Questions

Is YWAM one denomination?

No — it’s interdenominational and focused on Jesus.

Is it only for young people?

People of many ages join! Hunger matters more than age.

What’s the schedule like?

Lecture phase + outreach phase.

Could This Be Your Next Step?

YWAM Salem is a place where people encounter God, find clarity, and grow in courage.
