YOM Teams — Youth on Mission

Exploring how YOM teams empower young people to take the gospel to the nations.

Missions · Youth · Global Movement

YOM (Youth on Mission) is a growing movement centered around Jesus, discipleship, and hands-on mission work. Their heartbeat is simple: equip young people to know God deeply and share His love boldly—locally and around the world.

What Makes YOM Unique?

YOM teams don’t just talk about missions—they live it. Whether through evangelism, mercy ministries, discipleship, or community outreach, young people step into real situations where God moves powerfully.

Training for Real-World Impact

Before going out, YOM members receive intentional training focused on prayer, evangelism, leadership, and the Word of God. The aim isn't just skill-building—it's transformation from the inside out.

Serving in Communities Worldwide

From urban outreaches to remote villages, YOM teams serve wherever God opens a door. Their approach is simple: listen to God, love people, and share the gospel with boldness.

What YOM Teams Are Known For:
  • Bold evangelism and outreach
  • Genuine discipleship and mentorship
  • Cross-cultural ministry experience
  • Raising up young leaders for Kingdom impact

Ready to Join a YOM Team?

If you're hungry for adventure with God and ready to step out in faith, YOM Teams may be your next step. God is raising up a generation that isn’t afraid to go wherever He leads—and you might be part of it.
