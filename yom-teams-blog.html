YOM Teams — Youth On Mission Around the World

A fresh look at the ministry, movement, and momentum of YOM Teams.

Global Missions · Youth · Evangelism

Youth On Mission (YOM) is a movement empowering the next generation to carry the love of Jesus into cities, nations, and unreached places. Through discipleship, worship, outreach, and creative evangelism, YOM Teams are making the gospel visible—one heart, one family, one community at a time.

What Makes YOM Teams Unique?

YOM isn’t just an organization; it’s a heartbeat. A family of young believers stepping boldly into mission, discipleship, and community transformation.

1. Youth-led, Jesus-centered

Young people lead the charge with passion, creativity, and courage.

2. Mission-focused

Teams travel locally and globally to share the gospel, serve practically, and reveal the love of God.

3. Community-driven

YOM teams build relationships and partner with local ministries, churches, and leaders.

4. Globally connected

No matter where a YOM team goes—cities, villages, or nations—they are part of a worldwide family reaching the world together.

Ready to Join the Movement?

If God is pulling on your heart to go, serve, or step out in faith—YOM Teams are a powerful place to begin your journey.
