Exploring YOM Teams — Youth On Mission

A Kingdom Market spotlight on adventure, discipleship, and global impact.

If you’ve been searching for a missions experience that blends bold faith, real adventure, and practical outreach, then YOM Teams (Youth On Mission) might be exactly what you’ve been praying for.

What Is YOM Teams?

YOM is all about sending youth and young adults into the nations with a simple mission: Share Jesus. Serve faithfully. Love boldly. Teams travel to cities, villages, and communities worldwide bringing hope, energy, and the message of the Gospel with fresh passion.

What Makes YOM Special?

1. A Focus on Youth Empowerment

YOM believes God speaks to and moves through young people — not someday, but right now. Students discover their gifts, learn to hear God’s voice, and serve courageously.

2. Hands-On Missions

Expect full days of serving communities, leading kids programs, street outreach, worship nights, prayer walks, and supporting long-term missionaries.

3. Real Adventure + Real Discipleship

YOM creates the perfect blend of: • Deep encounters with God • Strong friendships • Cross-cultural missions • Exposure to global Christianity

Where Do YOM Teams Go?

Trips span continents — from Central America to Africa, Europe, and parts of Asia. Each location offers unique opportunities to serve, learn, and grow.

Should You Join a YOM Team?

If you're hungry for purpose, tired of staying comfortable, or ready to experience the heart of God in a new way — YOM Teams might be your next step.
